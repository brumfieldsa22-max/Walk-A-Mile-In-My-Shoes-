Walk A Mile In My Shoes

This interactive narrative was developed as a final project for my 2026 Disability Studies curriculum. It translates the core concepts of access intimacy, agency, and neurodivergence into a digital experience.

Drawing from the works of Alice Wong, Sara Nović, Will Leitch, and Tyler Page, this game invites you to navigate systemic barriers and celebrate the resilience of the disability community.
